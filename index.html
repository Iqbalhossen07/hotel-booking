Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions our guests ask.

Our standard check-in time is at 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and is subject to availability.

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before the check-in date. Cancellations made within 48 hours of arrival will be subject to a charge equivalent to one night's stay.

Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel! We allow small pets (up to 20 lbs) for a nominal fee. Please inform us at the time of booking if you plan to bring a pet.

We offer complimentary on-site parking for all our guests. Valet parking services are also available for your convenience at an additional charge.
