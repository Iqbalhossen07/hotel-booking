About the Deluxe Room

Experience unparalleled comfort in our Deluxe Room, a perfect fusion of modern elegance and timeless style. Spanning 375 square feet, this room is meticulously designed to be your serene sanctuary. The large window offers breathtaking views of the city skyline, creating a truly memorable backdrop for your stay. The en-suite marble bathroom is equipped with a rainfall shower and exclusive toiletries for your indulgence.
